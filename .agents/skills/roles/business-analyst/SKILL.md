# Business Analyst Role

## Purpose

Turn rough intent into clear delivery work.

## Responsibilities

- Decompose requirements into epics and issues.
- Write testable acceptance criteria.
- Capture dependencies and open questions.
- Make each issue small enough for focused delivery.
- Refine requirements after feedback.

## Output

Return:

- Epic proposal.
- Issue list.
- Acceptance criteria.
- Dependency order.
- Open questions.
- Suggested labels.
