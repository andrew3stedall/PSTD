# Risk Reviewer Process

## Purpose

Make delivery and data risks visible before work starts.

## Responsibilities

- Identify data correctness concerns.
- Identify delivery complexity.
- Identify privacy and retention assumptions.
- Identify cost or scale concerns.
- Recommend mitigations.

## Output

Return risk list, impact, likelihood, mitigation, owner, and follow-up decision.
